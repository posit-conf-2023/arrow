Compute time for querying the 1.15 billion rows:

nyc_taxi |> 
  filter(year == 2019) |>
  group_by(month) |>
  summarize(longest_trip = max(trip_distance, na.rm = TRUE)) |>
  arrange(month) |> 
  collect() |> 
  system.time()
   user  system elapsed 
  2.742   0.321   0.601
